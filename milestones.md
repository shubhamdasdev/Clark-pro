# Milestones — May 2026 → Dec 2027

Combined timeline across both workstreams. Targets are honest, not aspirational — designed so that if you hit them, the next phase is reachable. If you miss them, they're a signal to diagnose, not quit.

## Phase Map

| Phase | Window | Short-form (followers) | Long-form (newsletter) | Pipeline state |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **0. Pipeline build** | May 2026, weeks 1–2 | 0 → 100 | 0 → 30 (seed list from DMs) | Dashboard up, voice cloned, first avatar test reel |
| **1. Foundation** | May–early Jun 2026 | 100 → 1K | 30 → 100 | First 30 reels + 30 carousels + 4 newsletters shipped |
| **2. Iteration** | Jun–Jul 2026 | 1K → 8K | 100 → 250 | Drop paid tools (HeyGen → open-source). One short-form video hits. |
| **3. Compounding** | Aug–Oct 2026 | 8K → 30K | 250 → 800 | First viral loop. Recurring series ("Cortex Diary," "AI Built This"). 1 Medium accepted into publication. |
| **4. Series & Brand** | Nov 2026–Feb 2027 | 30K → 65K | 800 → 1.8K | Email funnel from short-form active. Speaking/podcast invites starting. |
| **5. Push** | Mar–Dec 2027 | 65K → 100K | 1.8K → 5K+ | Collabs, 2–3 more viral loops. Inbound career/Cortex opportunities. |

## Hard Targets

### Short-Form (TikTok + IG)
- **Week 4:** ≥1 video over 5K views OR retention >60% on 3+ videos
- **Week 8:** 1K followers, one video over 25K views
- **Month 6 (Oct 2026):** 30K followers
- **Month 12 (Apr 2027):** 65K followers — reassess positioning if under 35K
- **Dec 2027:** 100K target

### Long-Form
- **Day 14:** First newsletter sent. ≥30 subs. ≥3 LinkedIn posts shipped.
- **Day 30:** 4 newsletters, 12 LinkedIn posts, 1 Medium published. 50–100 subs.
- **Day 60:** 1 LinkedIn post over 10K impressions OR 1 Medium accepted into a publication.
- **Month 6:** 500–800 newsletter subs, first inbound (job/podcast/speaking).
- **Month 12:** 1.8K newsletter subs, regular publication acceptance.
- **Dec 2027:** 5K+ newsletter subs.

### Pipeline
- **Day 14:** finished-reel time ≤20 min human work. Zero pipeline-step failures in last 3 runs.
- **Day 30:** 30 reels + 30 carousels live. External spend ≤$30.
- **Day 60:** Open-source avatar swap-in tested. Spend tracking under $80.
- **Month 4:** Cumulative spend ≤$100. **If not, drop HeyGen immediately.**

## Honesty Floor

If month-3 numbers (Aug 2026) show <2K short-form followers and zero video over 10K views, **don't quit — diagnose.** The fix is almost always hooks + first 3 seconds, not topic.

If month-12 numbers (Apr 2027) show <35K short-form followers, **revisit positioning** — likely too broad. Lean harder into the AI/Ambition lane and let lifestyle/dating recede.

If newsletter is stuck under 200 subs at Month 6, **focus on LinkedIn first** — newsletter growth without LinkedIn distribution is slow. Push 5×/week instead of 3× until subs grow.

## Inflection Points to Watch For

These are the moments when the plan should shift mode:

1. **First viral video (>100K views).** Pause new format experiments, study why it worked, and replicate the format 5–10x in the next 2 weeks while it's hot.
2. **First 1K newsletter subs.** Turn on Beehiiv referral mechanic. Add a simple lead magnet (Cortex behind-the-scenes PDF or "AI PM toolkit").
3. **First inbound career/podcast/Cortex opportunity.** Triage carefully — say no to most. Pick the one that compounds the brand most.
4. **Cumulative spend hits $80.** Force the open-source switch immediately even if HeyGen quality feels safer.

## Annual Reviews

- **End of Year 1 (Apr 2027):** are short-form numbers > 35K and newsletter > 1.5K? If yes, double down — bump cadence to 2 reels/day. If no, restructure positioning.
- **End of Year 2 (Dec 2027):** 100K target reached or not — what's next? Possibilities: paid newsletter tier, Cortex audience funnel, course on AI PM craft, advisor work.
