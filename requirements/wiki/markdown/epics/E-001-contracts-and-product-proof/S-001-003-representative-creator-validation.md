## Story

As a **creator**, I want to **complete observed Clark workflows with the representative Full-Week fixture**, so that **the team can prove Focus, Canvas, memory, provenance, and authority are understandable before expanding automation**.

## Goal

Deliver the observable capability described above through Clark's canonical workspace, with enough evidence to determine whether it is complete.

## Why / Rationale

The team can prove Focus, Canvas, memory, provenance, and authority are understandable before expanding automation. This story is separated from adjacent work so the board can report verified progress without treating an incomplete product epic as finished.

## Functional Requirements

- Run five observed walkthroughs using the committed protocol and record assistance, wrong turns, timing, and explanation quality.
- Redesign and retest every failed critical gate before signoff.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Given five representative creators complete the protocol, when results are synthesized, then every Canvas and memory comprehension gate has attributable evidence.
- [ ] Given any critical comprehension gate fails, when Ground status is recalculated, then signoff remains blocked until a redesigned path passes a follow-up run.

## Negative Scenarios

- A failed trust, evidence, compatibility, recovery, or policy check must leave the prior canonical state intact and expose a valid next action.

## Future Scope

- Scope not named in this story remains in sibling stories or a later release; this story does not absorb the full parent epic.

## Do Not Do

- In this story, do not infer completion from UI presence, documents, or mocked fixtures when the acceptance criteria require runtime or human evidence.
- Do not bypass Clark's event, permission, approval, provenance, or rollback contracts to make the happy path appear complete.
