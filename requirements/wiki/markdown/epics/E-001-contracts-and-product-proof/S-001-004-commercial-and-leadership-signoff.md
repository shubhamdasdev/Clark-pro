## Story

As a **workspace_admin**, I want to **record accountable owners, capacity, security approval, and a binding replacement signal**, so that **Ground closes only when the project has real organizational and commercial commitment**.

## Goal

Deliver the observable capability described above through Clark's canonical workspace, with enough evidence to determine whether it is complete.

## Why / Rationale

Ground closes only when the project has real organizational and commercial commitment. This story is separated from adjacent work so the board can report verified progress without treating an incomplete product epic as finished.

## Functional Requirements

- Assign named primary and backup owners with capacity and dependency commitments.
- Complete the design-partner and purchase-signal protocols without substituting compliments or hypothetical intent.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Given all required evidence is present, when each named leadership role signs the ledger, then Ground may transition to closed.
- [ ] Given any owner, capacity commitment, security proof, or binding purchase signal is absent, when the ledger is verified, then Ground remains open with a precise next action.

## Negative Scenarios

- A failed trust, evidence, compatibility, recovery, or policy check must leave the prior canonical state intact and expose a valid next action.

## Future Scope

- Scope not named in this story remains in sibling stories or a later release; this story does not absorb the full parent epic.

## Do Not Do

- In this story, do not infer completion from UI presence, documents, or mocked fixtures when the acceptance criteria require runtime or human evidence.
- Do not bypass Clark's event, permission, approval, provenance, or rollback contracts to make the happy path appear complete.
