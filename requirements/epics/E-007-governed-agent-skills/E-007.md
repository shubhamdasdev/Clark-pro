## Outcome

Creators can trust, invoke, revise, and roll back reusable procedures through explicit capability ceilings and evidence without allowing a Skill package to self-promote or expand authority.

## Success Metric

| Metric | Baseline | Target |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Skill revisions receiving undeclared capability authority | 0 | 0 |
| Production Skill invocations with complete receipts | 0; invocation is intentionally absent | 100% after invocation ships |
