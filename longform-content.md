# Long-Form Content — Newsletter, Medium, LinkedIn

The long-form workstream builds **authority, network, and an owned audience** — the things that actually pay off in AI/PM career terms (job offers, advisor gigs, fundraising for Cortex, paid speaking). Short-form drives reach; long-form drives leverage.

## Goal

By end of 2027:
- **5–7K newsletter subscribers** (Beehiiv)
- **20K+ LinkedIn followers**
- **Regular Medium publication acceptance**
- **Inbound career / Cortex opportunities driven by long-form**

## Topics (Three Pillars)

1. **AI PM journey & work** — what shipping AI products actually looks like. Insider POV, not theory. Cortex build journal, lessons from day-job, postmortems.
2. **AI advancements decoded for PMs** — translate new model releases / techniques (RAG, agents, evals, fine-tuning) into "what this means for your roadmap."
3. **ML ideas in product management** — applying ML thinking to PM craft itself: experimentation, opportunity sizing, prioritization frameworks, eval-driven product dev.

Every piece must clearly fall in one pillar. No "general productivity" content — that's noise here.

## Channels

- **LinkedIn** — primary. Highest-leverage for a PM. AI/PM audience is concentrated here.
- **Newsletter (Beehiiv free tier)** — owned audience, deliverable to inbox. Best long-term asset.
- **Medium** — monthly long-form for SEO + indie tech / engineer crowd.

**Budget impact: $0 incremental.** All free tiers (Beehiiv free up to 2.5K subs; LinkedIn and Medium free).

## Cadence

| Channel | Frequency | Length | Day |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | 3×/week | 150–300 words | Mon, Wed, Fri AM |
| Newsletter | Weekly | 600–900 words | Sat AM |
| Medium | Monthly | 1500–2500 words | Last Sunday of month |

Total: ~17 pieces/month, all sourced from the same weekly note corpus.

## Atomic Content System (one input → many outputs)

```
[Daily 5-min note]   → personal Obsidian/Apple Notes "thinking log"
        ↓
[Saturday: weekly digest in Claude Code]
        ↓
   ┌────┴────┐
   ▼         ▼
Newsletter   3 LinkedIn posts (excerpts/angles)
   ↓
[Monthly: pick strongest theme, expand to Medium long-form]
   ↓
Medium → 1 LinkedIn post (linking to it) + 1–2 short-form reel scripts (Bucket 1)
```

This is what makes the cadence sustainable on top of everything else. Same idea, different format/depth per channel — never a copy-paste repost.

### Crossover with Short-Form Pipeline
- LinkedIn AI/PM posts feed directly into Bucket 1 (Ambition/AI) reel scripts.
- Medium long-form → break into 5-slide IG carousel + a "Cortex Diary" reel.
- One idea, repurposed across short + long form, is the leverage.

## LinkedIn Strategy

LinkedIn is the highest-ROI channel because the audience (other PMs, eng leaders, AI hiring managers, VCs) directly drives career upside — separately from follower count.

### Format mix (rotate across 3/week)
- **Insight post** — one observation from the week, 5–8 short paragraphs, no hashtag spam.
- **Carousel/document post** — 8–10 slide PDF (LinkedIn boosts these). Generated by the same `make_carousel.py` script as IG carousels, repurposed.
- **Personal milestone / build update** — Cortex shipping moment, lesson from a launch.

### Hooks that work (different from TikTok)
- "After shipping AI features for 18 months, here's what I'd tell my past self"
- "Most PMs are using LLMs wrong in their roadmap planning"
- "We killed a feature this week. Here's why."
- "RAG is dead — what AI PMs should care about instead in 2026"

### Don't
- Generic "10 lessons from..." listicles
- Motivational quotes
- AI hype
- The audience smells it.

## Newsletter Strategy (Beehiiv)

### Format (consistent every week)
- **Title:** specific and benefit-driven, not clever.
- **Cold open** — 2–3 sentences, the week's main idea.
- **The deep-dive** — 400–600 words on one topic from the week.
- **Three quick hits** — 50 words each on AI/ML news interpreted for PMs.
- **What I'm building** — Cortex update, one paragraph.
- **Reading queue** — 2–3 links worth your time.

### Goals
- **500 subs by Month 6**
- **2K subs by Month 12**

First 100 come from manual outreach (people you've talked to in AI/PM circles + LinkedIn promotion).

### Beehiiv referral mechanic
Turn it on at 100 subs. Best subscriber-acquisition lever on the platform.

## Medium Strategy

### Cadence
1 deep-dive/month, 1500–2500 words. Quality over volume.

### Topics that work
- **Frameworks and mental models** — e.g., "How I prioritize AI features when every team wants AI"
- **Postmortems** — e.g., "We shipped an LLM feature that flopped — here's what we learned"
- **Technical-but-accessible** — e.g., "Eval-driven product development for PMs"

### Distribution flywheel
Publish to Medium → cross-post excerpt + link on LinkedIn → mention in next newsletter → 1–2 reel scripts pulled from the article.

### Submit to publications
- Towards Data Science
- Product Coalition
- Better Programming

First-time submissions take 1–2 weeks. Once you have 1 accepted, future ones go faster.

## First 14 Days — Long-Form Setup

Runs in **parallel** with the avatar pipeline build. Long-form needs much less weekly time once set up (~3 hrs/week).

### Days 1–2
- Beehiiv account + welcome email written.
- Medium profile (photo, one-line bio, link to Beehiiv).
- LinkedIn bio refreshed: AI PM + Cortex + Chicago + newsletter link.
- Set up note capture (Obsidian vault or Apple Notes folder named `creator-notes`).

### Days 3–7
- Daily 5-min note dump.
- **Day 5:** write & ship first LinkedIn post (any pillar).
- **Day 7:** write & send first newsletter to seed list (DM 30 friends/colleagues to subscribe — that's your launch list).

### Days 8–14
- LinkedIn 3×: Mon/Wed/Fri.
- Newsletter Saturday.
- Start drafting first Medium piece (publish end of Month 1).

## Verification

- **Day 14:** First newsletter sent. ≥30 subs. ≥3 LinkedIn posts shipped.
- **Day 30:** 4 newsletters, 12 LinkedIn posts, 1 Medium published. 50–100 subs.
- **Day 60:** 1 LinkedIn post over 10K impressions OR 1 Medium accepted into a publication.
- **Month 6:** 500–800 newsletter subs, regular publication acceptance, first inbound (job/podcast/speaking).
- **Spend KPI:** $0 spent on long-form by Month 6.
